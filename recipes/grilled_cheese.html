Grilled Cheese

Description

Learn how to make a grilled cheese sandwich in a nonstick pan with buttered bread and American Cheddar for a classic hot sandwich.

Ingredients

  • bread
  • butter
  • sliced cheese

Steps

  1. Gather ingredients
  2. butter bread
  3. heat up a nonstick skillet
  4. place the butter side down with 1 slice of cheese on top of the bread
  5. butter a second slice and put the butter-side facing up on top of the cheese
  6. cook until lightly brown on one side, flip over and continue cooking the other side
  7. serve
