Dumplings

Description

A dumpling is a small mass of dough that can be boiled, fried, baked, or steamed. There are a ton of dumpling varieties out there, from pork- and cabbage-filled Chinese dumplings to cheesy Polish dumplings.

Ingredients

  • flour
  • baking powder
  • white sugar
  • salt
  • butter
  • milk
  • minced meat

Steps

  1. Gather ingredients
  2. Stir flour, baking powder, sugar and salt in a bowl
  3. Cut in butter until mixture is crumbly. Stir in milk and mix.
  4. Wrap about a tablespoons worth of meat into the batter
  5. Tie a knot at the top with the dough/batter
  6. drop the dumpling into soup, cover and simmer for 15 minutes
  7. serve
